About Dr. Amutha Rani Ganapath

Dr. Amutharani G is a highly esteemed Consultant specializing in Obstetrics & Gynaecology, known for her comprehensive expertise in reproductive health and women’s care. She completed her MBBS at Al-Ameen Medical College, Bijapur in 2005, followed by an MD in Obstetrics and Gynaecology from PSG Institute of Medical Science & Research in 2013. With over 18+ years of dedicated practice, Dr. Amutharani has garnered extensive experience and honed her skills in various aspects of women’s healthcare.

Currently serving as a Consultant at Iswarya Fertility Centre in Adyar, Chennai, Dr. Amutharani G provides a wide range of services tailored to meet the diverse needs of her patients. Her specialization includes obstetric care, gynecological consultations, infertility evaluation and treatment, menopausal management, laparoscopic gynecological surgeries, and comprehensive postpartum care and counselling.

Dr. Amutharani G approach to patient care is characterized by compassion, proficiency, and a commitment to delivering optimal health outcomes. She emphasizes personalized treatment plans that consider each patient’s unique medical history and preferences, ensuring they receive the highest quality care possible.

Her extensive experience in managing pregnancies, treating infertility issues, performing laparoscopic surgeries, and guiding women through menopausal transitions has earned her a reputation for excellence among her peers and patients a like.

Dr. Amutharani G actively engages in professional development and is dedicated to staying abreast of the latest advancements in obstetrics, gynaecology, and reproductive medicine. Her commitment to ongoing education and patient advocacy underscores her role as a trusted healthcare provider in the community.

For individuals seeking expert care in obstetrics, gynaecology, and infertility treatments in Chennai, Dr. Amutharani G stands out as a compassionate specialist dedicated to enhancing women’s health and well-being.
